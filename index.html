The Undersold Score Formula

Our proprietary Undersold Score (USS) is a composite metric computed as:

USS = α·(E[R] − Ractual) + β·Vsentiment + γ·(1 − Smarket)
E[R] = Expected rating (category benchmark) Ractual = Observed star rating Vsentiment = Sentiment variance score Smarket = Market saturation index α, β, γ = Learned weights (gradient boosting)

A higher USS indicates a product where customer expectations significantly exceed current offerings — the ideal entry point for a quality-focused dropshipper.

Trending Rank Algorithm

1
Velocity Detection

Measures rate-of-change in search volume, social mentions, and purchase frequency over rolling 7/30-day windows.

2
Cross-Platform Correlation

Correlates TikTok virality signals with Amazon BSR movements using a Granger causality test to identify leading indicators.

3
Momentum Scoring

Applies an exponential smoothing model with decay factor λ to weight recent signals more heavily than historical data.

4
Rank Assignment

Products are ranked by composite momentum score, updated every 4 minutes with confidence bounds from a Monte Carlo simulation.
